PROGRAM OVERVIEW

Industry Training, Without Students Leaving Campus

Our In-Campus Training Program brings industry-focused learning directly to educational institutions, enabling students to gain practical knowledge, technical expertise, and hands-on project experience within their own campus environment.

Through expert-led sessions, real-world case studies, and emerging technologies, the program is built around your existing academic calendar, lab infrastructure and department goals, so it integrates with coursework rather than competing with it.

PROGRAM TRACKS

How An Engagement Comes Together

From first conversation to certified students, here's the typical flow.

01
Needs Discussion With Department

We sit with your faculty coordinators to understand student strength, existing syllabus and skill gaps.

02
Curriculum Customization

Module list, hours and tools are finalized to align with your academic calendar and lab availability.

03
On-Campus Delivery

Trainers conduct sessions in your classrooms/labs with a mix of theory, live coding and case studies.

04
Hands-on Project Work

Students apply what they've learned to a mini project, individually or in teams, under trainer guidance.

05
Assessment & Certification

A final assessment determines certification, with batch-wise performance shared with the institution.
