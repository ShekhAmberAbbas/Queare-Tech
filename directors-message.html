MESSAGE FROM THE DIRECTOR

Building Careers Beyond Classrooms

Dear Academic Leaders, Industry Partners, and Esteemed Stakeholders, Over the past 17 years, I have had the privilege of working across diverse domains including software development, technology consulting, training and skill development, strategic planning, organizational growth, and This journey has provided me with a unique perspective on one of the most critical challenges facing our nation today—the gap between academic learning and industry expectations. Yashi Asthana Director Capacity Building & Strategic Growth Quaere Etechnologies Pvt Ltd As technology continues to redefine industries and careers at an unprecedented pace, institutions and organizations must evolve beyond conventional approaches. Today's students require more than degrees; they need practical exposure, industry-relevant skills, technological adaptability, professional confidence, and a mindset of continuous learning. Similarly, industries seek talent that can contribute from day one and grow alongside emerging innovations. At Quaere, we are committed to building a strong bridge between education and industry through technology-driven solutions, capacity-building initiatives, industry-oriented training programs, digital learning platforms, research collaborations, and workforce development strategies. Our vision is not limited to imparting skills; it is to create an ecosystem where knowledge translates into employability, innovation, and sustainable career growth. Having worked closely with educational institutions, corporate organizations, government projects, and technology teams throughout my professional journey, I strongly believe that meaningful transformation can only be achieved through collaboration. Educational institutions possess the power to shape future talent, while industry brings practical insights and real-world challenges. When both work together, remarkable outcomes become possible. As Director (Capacity Building & Strategic Growth), my focus is to strengthen these partnerships, create impactful learning opportunities, develop future-ready talent, and contribute towards building a skilled workforce capable of meeting the demands of a rapidly evolving global economy. I look forward to working together in creating innovative pathways for learning, growth, and professional excellence. At Quaere, we do not merely prepare students for jobs—we prepare them for careers, leadership, and lifelong success
