PROGRAM OVERVIEW

Learn By Building, Not Just Watching

Our Summer Training Program is designed for students who want more than a certificate — it gives hands-on industry exposure, real-world projects, and expert mentorship to enhance technical skills and career readiness.

Interns work in small batches under the direct supervision of trainers who are practicing developers and engineers, so the projects, code reviews and feedback resemble what happens inside a real product team rather than a classroom exercise.

CURRICULUM

What You'll Work Through

A structured path from fundamentals to a deployable final project.

01
Orientation & Track Selection

Tooling setup, team introductions, and choosing a specialization track based on interest and aptitude.

02
Core Skill Building

Guided modules in your chosen track — web development, AI/ML, data science or cloud fundamentals.

03
Live Project Assignment

Interns are placed into small teams and assigned a real or simulated client-style project brief.

04
Build, Review, Iterate

Weekly mentor check-ins, code reviews and milestone demos to keep the project on track.

05
Final Presentation & Certification

Interns present their finished project to a panel and receive their verified completion certificate.
